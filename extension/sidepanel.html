How it works

  1. Add an image-capable provider once at readycode.ai/byok (OpenRouter recommended — one key covers Nano Banana, GPT-Image, Flux…).
  2. Sign in to ReadyCode in your browser. The extension picks up your session automatically.
  3. Right-click any product image on the web → Grab this image with ReadyCode ImageKit, or paste a URL.
  4. Pick a style, hit Generate. Your AI key bills you directly. ReadyCode never touches an image model.
  5. Save up to 100 images / 200 MB free in your Library — accessible from any device at readycode.ai/imagekit/library.

Privacy: scrapes only fetch public page HTML. Your BYOK key never leaves ReadyCode's vault. Generated images live in a private storage bucket scoped to your account.
